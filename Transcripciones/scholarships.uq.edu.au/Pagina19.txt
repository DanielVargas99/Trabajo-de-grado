scholarship in quantitative biology 

open to domestic and international students who have received an unconditional offer to study the master of quantitative biology program at the university of queensland on a full time basis







 



wensley sporting scholarship 

financial assistance to support students with financial need that are competing at an elite level in a recognised sport







 



uq academic scholarships  

support for domestic and international school leavers and gap year students who have demonstrated significant academic leadership andor community service achievements
