Think Big Postgraduate Scholarships

Prospective international students can apply for funding towards the cost of tuition fees.


Scholarships will be available for our qualifying courses starting September 2022.

EligibilityYou can apply if you:

have applied to start a one-year full-time Master's programme at the University of Bristol in September 2022
are classed as an overseas student for fee purposes.

Qualifying programmes
Any one-year, full-time Master's programme.
